What are the benefits to CRB membership?

  • Open Web Device CRB provides operators and device vendors an independent third party certification for devices to meet API compliance and minimum performance benchmarks. This ensures a consistent experience, unleashing the full power of the Web on these devices. This is equally critical for users and developers. Towards that goal all industry members, however big or small, can benefit with CRB as a common forum to establish such compliance criteria.
  • It provides a platform (as opposed to individual efforts of companies) to plan and execute the necessary steps needed to support flourishing Open Web App ecosystem without fragmentation.
  • CRB membership provides access to participation in meetings.
  • Statutory membership additionally provides voting rights on key propositions.
