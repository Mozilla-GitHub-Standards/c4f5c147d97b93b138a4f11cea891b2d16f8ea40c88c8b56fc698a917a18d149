What are the benefits to CRB membership?

  • Independent third party certification for devices to meet API compliance and minimum performance benchmarks. This ensures a consistent experience, unleashing the full power of the Web on these devices. This is equally critical for users and developers. All industry members, however big or small, benefit from using CRB as a common forum to establish such compliance criteria.
  • A unified platform to plan and execute the necessary steps needed to support a flourishing Open Web App ecosystem without fragmentation
  • Access to participate in CRB meetings (All members)
  • Voting rights on key propositions (Statutory members)
  • Eligibility for board seats (Statutory members only)
  • Chair Steering Committee or Working Groups (Statutory members)
  • Participation in Steering Committee or Working Groups
  • A voice in how a device is qualified for markets worldwide
  • Early product alignment with pending industry certification criteria
